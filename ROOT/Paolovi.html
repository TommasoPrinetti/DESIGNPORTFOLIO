Rethinking the brand system

The previous situation was heavily confused and there was no brand approach that could guide the school into its different artifacts. We started interviewing a series of stakeholders to understand the actual needs of a future communication. Then, we went more and more concrete on the output providing templates and a value set that could follow the pillars of the educational method of Paolo VI.

Web-design as a tool

The majority of the time we spent in this project was to understand a clear architecture that could give them a set of easily accessible pages. What came out is a well-structured website that hosts all the different souls of the school itself: from the burocrathic ones to the user-generated content.
